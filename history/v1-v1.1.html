Privacy Policy for Playexa Video Player

Effective Date: 21 April 2026

Playexa Video Player is a media player application designed to play videos stored locally on your device. We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing a transparent user experience.

1. Information Collection

Playexa does not collect personal data or share any personal information.

The app does not require:

  • Account registration or login
  • An active internet connection for its core functionality
  • Any personal user data

2. Permissions Usage

To function as a video player, Playexa requires standard permissions to access the media files stored on your Android device:

  • READ_MEDIA_VIDEO (Android 13 and above)
  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (Android 12 and below)

These permissions are strictly used only to access and display local video files.

We do not extract or upload your video files to any remote server or cloud network.

3. Local Processing and File Management

  • All data processing occurs locally on your device.
  • The app allows users to delete or rename video files.
  • Files are modified only after explicit user action and confirmation.
  • No files are modified, deleted, or altered without your direct consent.

4. Data Sharing & Third-Party Services

Playexa does not require an active internet connection for its core functionality. No data is transmitted over the internet, and we do not use background analytics, tracking networks, or third-party tracking services.

Playexa does not share any data with advertising companies or other third parties. We do not sell, rent, or monetize any user data.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally if Android permission requirements change. Any modifications will be reflected on this page.
