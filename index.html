Privacy Policy for PlayExa Video Player

Effective Date: 18 July 2026

PlayExa Video Player ("the App", "we", "our", or "us") is a media player application designed to play videos stored locally on your device. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe, transparent user experience.

This Privacy Policy explains our data practices. By using PlayExa Video Player, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

1. Information Collection and Use

PlayExa Video Player is designed with a privacy-first approach. We strictly do not collect, store, or process any personal data.

The App explicitly DOES NOT:

  • Require user account registration or login.
  • Collect personal information, location data, or device identifiers.
  • Collect crash reports, diagnostics, analytics, or usage statistics.
  • Upload, transmit, or back up your media files to any remote server or cloud network.
  • Use Firebase, Crashlytics, or similar backend reporting tools.

2. Advertising and Third-Party Tracking

To guarantee a secure and private experience, PlayExa Video Player does not use any advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or third-party tracking technologies.

We do not sell, rent, monetize, or share user data with advertising companies, data brokers, or any other third parties.

3. Permissions Usage

To function strictly as a local video player, PlayExa Video Player requires standard Android permissions to discover and display media files stored on your Android device:

  • READ_MEDIA_VIDEO (Android 13 and above)
  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (Android 12 and below)

These permissions are used exclusively to access and display your local video files within the App.

These permissions are requested only to enable media browsing and playback. The App never uploads or shares your files with any external server.

4. Local Processing and File Management

  • Zero Cloud Processing: All media rendering and data processing occur entirely locally on your device.
  • File Operations: The App allows you to rename or delete your video files. Files are modified only after explicit user action and confirmation.
  • No files are altered, removed, or shared automatically or without your direct consent.

5. Network Access

The core video playback functionality of PlayExa Video Player operates entirely offline using local video files stored on your device.

Optional user-initiated actions, such as clicking a button to contact support, will open your device’s default email client. Sending this email may require an active internet connection provided by that selected application. The App itself does not independently communicate over the internet.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in Android permission requirements or Google Play Developer Policies. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
